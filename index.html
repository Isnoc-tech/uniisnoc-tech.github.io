About Us

As a pioneer in the furniture industry Unisonic boasts of a proud history of over 25 years. Trusted over many generations who enjoyed the comforts and exclusivity, Unisonic strives to perfect versatility and style.

Unisonic underwent a restructuring in its management and is presently known as Unisonic Interior Solutions (Pvt) Ltd under the brand name Unisonic & Dwara Interiors (Pvt) Ltd is the Marketing arm of the Unisonic Interior Solutions (Pvt) Ltd.

Our furniture is designed and manufactured using the latest state of the art machinery and technology to precise and stringent quality standards. Our workforce is skilled and trained to carry out any manufacturing task to precision detail, thus ensuring you of furniture and designs of the highest quality, finish and style.
